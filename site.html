this site is under active development as of today: click on this sentence to view the live project.

The domain I use for my personal website has its registration renewed at the end of May every year. On its anniversary, I decided to rewrite the site from scratch.

There are several Easter Eggs being added to the site on a semi-regular basis. One can simply go through the source code to find them, but I believe that the satisfaction of stumbling across one at random far ooutweighs the satisfaction of a passionate egg-hunt.

In addition to using plain HTML and CSS, I built this website using the following:
  • Eleventy, a static site generator
  • Netlify, a dynamic web-hosting platform
